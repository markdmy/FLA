PARTNERSHIP


Free Laundry Access has partnered with laundromat owners who share the same mission of providing access to clean laundry to those individuals and families facing financial challenges. Clean laundry should be accessible to all.

Are you a laundromat owner and share this vision? If so, please fill out the form and we would love to work with you to create an Event hosted at your location.
