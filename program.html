OUR PROGRAM

Free Laundry Access Program is a program to help individuals and families who are experiencing financial challenges living in Canada with access to clean laundry free of cost.

HOW IT WORKS

Eligible residents will receive a credit at each event date for Free Laundry Access, which will be based on number of individuals within a given household. Free Laundry Access program is ONLY offered on announced Event dates, times and at specific locations. Outside of the Event dates and times, unfortunately the program is NOT available Remember, each Event is coordinated with our laundromat partners in advance in order to provide the Free Laundry Access program and services. Outside of the Event date our laundromat partners do not have the resources required to run the program. Amount of credit per event is set at a maximum of 4 loads or 30 pounds of laundry per individual member within a household.

For Example, A household containing:

1 Individual : 4 loads or 30 pounds of laundry

2 Individuals: 8 loads or 60 pounds of laundry

3 Individuals: 12 loads or 90 pounds of laundry

4 Individuals: 16 loads or 120 pounds of laundry
