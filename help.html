DONATION


Give back to your amazing community by volunteering at Free Laundry Access! Each Free Laundry Access events requires many hands to make it successful. It involves the contributions of Free Laundry Access Inc., our volunteers, sponsors, partners, and our host laundromats all working together. Volunteering to help others is not only a rewarding experience which words cannot describe but it makes such a tremendous difference in the lives of individuals and families you are helping, as well as making your community a better place to live.

As a volunteer you not only give your time, effort and talents but also a part of your heart, your generosity, your compassion, and your empathy to those who are less privileged. You are helping so many people in need of this necessary service of gaining access to clean laundry across Canada.

It is you, the volunteers, who make the world a better place, create a community with warmth and belonging for all, a place to call home. Help Free Laundry Access continue serving the communities across Canada gain access to clean laundry. Come volunteer with us and make a positive difference in your community.
